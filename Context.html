Our football development program

The Football program is designed for children between the ages of 5 and 18 years irrespective of their individual abilities. Participants will be given a solid foundation in football and taught the right techniques to help them make the most of their profound passion for football in an enjoyable and friendly environment.

Our Football Development program is structured across different levels of development, from Foundation to Advanced football classes, built on expertly designed curriculums, which take players on a journey, promoting passion and love for football as they grow and improve, with skills and positive experiences.

We are affiliated to Team360, Box2Box Fc and Starbase FC of Lagos.

    Introduction level

    This program is for boys and girls between 4 and 7 years old. It focusses on the introductory principles of football development. The players are required to put in between 2-4 hours of football per week. They will also be introduced to the Eagles League, an internal football league program where all our players in this category are placed in teams that will play against each other within and across all our centers. The focus pillars are Physical, Technical and Mental

    Foundation level

    This program is for boys and girls between 8 and 11 years old. It focusses on the foundational principles of football development. The players are required to put in between 3-5 hours of football per week. They will also be introduced to the Eagles League, an internal football league program where all our players in this category are placed in teams that will play against each other within and across all our centers. Players here will be exposed to competitive games.

    Pre-academy level

    This program is for boys between 12 and 15 years old. It focusses on intensive training drills as it prepares players for semi professional football. The players are required to put in 5- 7 hours of football per week and Will be exposed to friendly games with other academies

    Girls development program(Pre-Academy Level)

    This program is for girls between 12 and 19 years old. It focusses on intensive training drills as it prepares players for up to semi professional football. The players are required to put in 5-7 hours of football per week

    The LFC FIVE Pillars of Sports Development

    Physical

    In the journey of development, our curriculum focuses on building and enhancing motor skills such as endurance, speed, power, agility, among others. These are some of the areas we will develop players to prepare them for the intensity of the game.

    Technical

    We recognize that individuals have varying levels of talent and ability. Our curriculum is designed to fan that spark of talent, no matter the level of skill, on a journey of development that shows visible and significant progress in the playerâ€™s ability

    Tactical

    An individual may possess skills but lack the knowledge or awareness of when and where to apply that skill in real-world. We teach and guide every player in our program to develop the ability to read and understand the flow of the game and apply themselves appropriately. (field).

    Mantal

    Sports is not just a game of physical strength and ability. Our focus here is to teach our players personal leadership Skills for success on & off the pitch; build confidence and develop their minds to break through mental barriers so they can progress and perform up to their full potential.

    Adaptability

    Ambitious people constantly strive to adapt to and excel in different environments. Our end goal is to develop players capable of competing and holding their own as professional footballers wherever they find themselves; we will engage players on foreign trips and tournaments to enhance their game abilities, confidence and social interaction skills.
